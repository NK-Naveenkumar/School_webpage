         What began as a small school in with a 9 students in a small village in West Godavari has created a sensation in the field of education by winning national and state awards as well as ranks in JEE-IIT and EAMCET. The very name Sasi instills confidence in the minds of many anxious parents aspiring students in and around the Coastal Districts of Andhra Pradesh with its strong roots in West and East Godavari districts is imparting. Sasi has created its own brand image for the values it follows. Sasi Educational Society with its strong roots in West and East Godavari districts is imparting quality education for over 38 years. The total students strength of all the institutions run by the society is about 16000+

Hostel Facility

SASI has separate hostel facility for boys and girls. The hostel is located in the college premises itself. The mess is spacious with a dining hall and kitchen. Separate seating arrangements are made for both boys and girls. Full time resident wardens are available to take care of hostellers and to maintain the discipline. A standby power generator is available with full time attendant in the hostel to operate during power failures. Life in hostel campus helps the students not only to study but also to socialize. SASI provides a comfortable and safe accommodation to all its students. The students accommodated in the campus are encouraged to use the gymnasium, playground and all indoor gaming facilities. The hostel, in addition, has a general store for provisions required by the students. Study hours will be conducted exclusively to the hostellers after college hours to improve their academic performance.
